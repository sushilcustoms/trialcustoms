About Customs Indore

Customs Indore, under the Central Board of Indirect Taxes and Customs (CBIC), Ministry of Finance, plays a crucial role in trade facilitation, revenue collection, and law enforcement in Madhya Pradesh. Established to regulate international trade, it oversees Inland Container Depots (ICDs), Air Cargo complexes, and international airports such as Devi Ahilyabai Holkar International Airport, Indore. Customs Indore ensures smooth import-export operations, enforces the Customs Act, 1962, and combats smuggling and illegal trade to safeguard national security. It has embraced digital initiatives like ICEGATE for seamless, paperless customs clearance, supporting the Ease of Doing Business initiative. Additionally, it conducts awareness programs and actively participates in events like International Customs Day and Hindi Pakhwada. By integrating modern trade policies and technology, Customs Indore continues to streamline operations, enhancing efficiency while ensuring compliance with international trade laws.
