History

The Office of the Commissioner of Customs in Indore was established on January 15, 2018, as a subordinate office to the Central Board of Indirect Taxes and Customs (CBIC). This establishment reflects Indore's growing significance in trade and commerce, necessitating a dedicated customs administration to manage and facilitate international trade operations in the region.
